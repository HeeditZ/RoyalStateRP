In-Game Rules

1. No Random Deathmatch (RDM) - You must have a valid roleplay reason for killing another player.
2. No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM) - Using vehicles to kill others without valid RP is prohibited.
3. No Metagaming - Using out-of-character information in-character is not allowed.
4. No Powergaming - Forcing RP or using unrealistic actions that remove others’ RP freedom is banned.
5. No Cop Baiting - Do not provoke law enforcement without valid RP reasons.
6. Fear RP - Respect life-threatening situations; act accordingly when at gunpoint or facing danger.
7. New Life Rule (NLR) - You may not return to the location of your death for at least 5 minutes.
8. No Exploiting or Cheating - Use of mods, cheats, or exploits will result in a ban.
9. Roleplay Your Character - Stay in character at all times unless in an OOC zone.
10. Respect Staff - Do not argue with staff decisions in-game, use the ticket system on Discord.

Discord Rules

Respect everyone. No hate speech, racism, or bullying.
Do not spam in any channels.
Use channels for their intended purpose only.
No advertising or self-promotion without permission.
Do not ping staff unnecessarily.
Follow Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
